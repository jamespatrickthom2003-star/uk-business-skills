Why this exists

UK business admin shouldn't be a subscription trap.

I'm a UK sole trader. ExternalOS is the catalogue I built because the category response to "I need to handle my own compliance" was a £19-a-month subscription that wants to live in your bank account forever, or an accountant at £200 an hour whose hour you can't afford. Neither answer is sized for someone running a small business on small margins.

UK business admin is genuinely complicated. That's why the category exists. But the pricing model isn't a function of complexity; it's a function of habit. Compliance answers don't change every month, and you shouldn't be billed every month for them. The mission is to make these tools accessible to anyone running a UK business, without the SaaS lock-in.

£9 to £49 per tool. Not per user, not per month, not per filing. Download the file, keep the file, and when HMRC rates change we ship the update.
