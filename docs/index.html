Why this exists

Built by a guy in Yorkshire who'd had enough Sundays on HMRC tabs.

I'm a sole trader myself. I'd spent too many Sundays on the same HMRC pages, the same 11pm panic before 31 January, the same "is this allowable?" question Googled four different ways. The category response is a £19-a-month subscription that wants to live in your bank account forever, or an accountant at £200 an hour whose hour you can't afford.

There was a third option nobody was selling. Do the work once. Package it as files. Sell it for the price of a takeaway. When the legislation moves, ship the update for free. The point of compliance software is not to charge you every month for the same answer; it is to give you the answer in a file you can keep.

I charge £9 to £49 per tool because the work is done. Not per user, not per month, not per filing. You download the file, you keep the file, and when the legislation moves I ship the update. That's the whole arrangement.
